CCPA Privacy Rights

As stipulated by the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), consumers residing in California are entitled to the following rights:

  • Knowledge of Data Collection: You have the right to be informed about the types of personal data collected about you, the sources of this data, and the purposes for which it is used.
  • Request for Data Deletion: You can request the deletion of your personal data from our records, subject to certain exceptions as outlined by the law.
  • Opt-Out of Data Sale: You have the option to opt out of the sale of your personal data to third parties. We respect your choice and will not sell your data if you exercise this right.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us. We are committed to addressing your requests and inquiries promptly and in compliance with the CCPA.

GDPR Data Protection Rights

In accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), users within the European Union (EU) and certain other jurisdictions enjoy the following data protection rights:

  • Access to Personal Data: You have the right to request access to the personal data we have collected about you. This includes information about how and why it is being used.
  • Correction of Inaccurate Data: If you believe that the personal data we hold about you is inaccurate or incomplete, you have the right to request its correction.
  • Data Erasure: Under specific conditions, you can request the deletion of your personal data from our records. This right is also known as the "right to be forgotten."
  • Processing Restriction Requests: You can request the limitation of processing your personal data, typically when you contest the accuracy of the data or when processing is unlawful.
  • Objecting to Processing: In certain cases, you have the right to object to the processing of your personal data, especially if it is being used for direct marketing or legitimate interests.
